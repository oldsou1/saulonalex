Cover Duo Based in Winnipeg - Playing from classics to pop hits!

We are Saulo & Alex!

An unconventional international music duo that randomly met each other in the cold lands of Canada. As music lovers, we're here to bring the world together with amazing covers of popular songs. Our repertoire is a melting pot of different genres and cultures, ranging from the old classics to the most recent pop hits and everything in between so there's something for everyone to enjoy. Beyond just genres, we connect with the audience in five different languages! It's our way of bridging cultures and making sure everyone feels the vibe. And hey, our playlist? It's always evolving. We're constantly adding new foreign tunes, because for us, music is about exploration and sharing the love.
